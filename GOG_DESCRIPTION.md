## About This Game

**Grow Your Fortune, One Bloom at a Time.**

*Coin Bloom* is a relaxing yet deeply strategic idle tycoon game where you nurture a humble gardening business into a global botanical empire. Start with a simple **Seed Cart** and work your way up to high-tech **Solar Blooms**, managing resources, automating production, and unlocking the secrets of infinite growth.

Whether you're an active clicker or a passive planner, *Coin Bloom* rewards your strategy. Watch your garden flourish as you unlock new ventures, hire skilled managers, and hit satisfying production milestones.

### Key Features

*   **From Seed to Solar:** Manage 10 distinct gardening ventures, including the charming **Flower Stall**, the bustling **Beehive**, the elegant **Vineyard**, and the futuristic **Botanical Lab**.
*   **Strategic Growth:** Balance your budget between buying more inventory and unlocking expensive upgrades. Costs rise as you grow‚Äîcan you optimize your spending for maximum efficiency?
*   **Automate Your Empire:** Tired of clicking? Hire dedicated **Managers** to run your businesses for you. Let the profits roll in while you sleep!
*   **Milestone Bonuses:** Hit production targets (25, 50, 100 owned) to trigger massive speed and profit multipliers. Every milestone is a celebration!
*   **Customizable Experience:** Play your way with built-in **Light and Dark themes** to suit your mood or time of day.
*   **Planned Features:** The roadmap includes a full Career mode, Prestige system (Investors), innovative Upgrades shop, and cloud saving capabilities.
